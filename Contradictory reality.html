Why do floods concentrate in the areas along the rivers?

From the map of crop distribution in Portugal, it can be seen that the areas where floods occurred were mostly in the rice-growing regions. First of all, rice cultivation in Portugal has always relied on waterlogging irrigation, so their terrain is inherently lower than that of other areas. On the other hand, the cultivation of rice and the use of its fertilizers have altered the soil structure here, reducing its ability to hold back floods, which in turn led to flooding.
