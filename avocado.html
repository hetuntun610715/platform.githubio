Aquaterra Avocado Plantation

Background Introduction

This avocado plantation is managed by Exponente Frugal Lda, a subsidiary of Aquaterra. The plantation is located in the Alentejo region of Portugal. It is situated within the t3 aquifer on the left bank of the Sado River and within two Natural 2000 protected areas. Currently, this area is facing significant environmental pressure. The project has undergone three assessment processes, but none of them have been approved yet. The report is still being revised and resubmitted.

Analysis of the Deficiencies in the EIA Document

1

screenshot of the content of the EIA document

In the EIA document, they stated that the impact of the herbicides and pesticides they used on the local area was insignificant. However, through an analysis of the local environmental conditions, it can be clearly seen from the data that there has already been nitrate pollution in the area. When new pesticides enter the soil and penetrate the aquifer, they will have a superimposed reaction with each other. But this environmental impact assessment document did not take into account this complex situation.
