Environmental Impact Assessment Shared Database

Subtitle / Concept: A participatory environmental archive

WEBSITE FUNCTION / BRIEF INTRODUCTION

This platform aims to connect and integrate the fragmented environmental issues hidden in the local area for analysis and presentation. It also creates an environmental impact assessment database that is jointly developed by local residents, farmers, members of environmental protection movements, and others, so that local farmers and residents can participate in the environmental risk assessment process together.
